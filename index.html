Toward System Intelligence

Our vision is a leap towards endogenous system intelligence — given high-level specifications (e.g., optimization objectives, correctness properties), system intelligence can drive continuous discovery of new opportunities and autonomously act on them.

Our Mission

System Intelligence is a research initiative focused on advancing AI's ability to design, implement, and operate next-generation computing systems. Unlike traditional AI approaches that primarily automate coding or bug fixing, System Intelligence represents a fundamental leap: AI that can reason about system architectures and protocols, evaluate trade-offs, apply timeless design principles, and create new abstractions that drive innovation.

However, despite the promise of this concept, the definition of System Intelligence has remained vague—slowing progress and limiting collaboration across the field. Our mission is to change that. We aim to sharpen the vision of System Intelligence and develop practical methods that empower AI to perform systems research and innovation with human-like creativity, rigor, and autonomy.
